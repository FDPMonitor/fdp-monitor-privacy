## Frequently Asked Questions

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → FDP Monitor → Cancel Subscription.

**How do I restore my purchase?**
Open FDP Monitor → tap "Restore Purchases" in the paywall screen.

**The app is not calculating correctly — what should I do?**
Verify your reporting time is in UTC and your UTC offset is set correctly in Settings.
