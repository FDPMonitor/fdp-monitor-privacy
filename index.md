# Privacy Policy — FDP Monitor

**Effective date: March 4, 2026**

This privacy policy applies to the FDP Monitor application (hereinafter "the App"), developed by prodavam-nemovitost.cz, s.r.o.. The privacy of your information is taken very seriously. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

## Data Controller

The data controller is prodavam-nemovitost.cz, s.r.o., e-mail: daniel.mikes@icloud.com. For the purposes of applicable data protection laws, including the GDPR (EU) 2016/679, the data controller determines the purposes and manner in which your data is processed.

## Data Collection

The App does not collect any personal data. All FDP calculations, settings, and entered values are processed and stored exclusively on your device. No data is transmitted to the developer's servers or any third-party servers.

## Automatically Collected Data

The App may automatically collect anonymous crash and error data through Apple's built-in services. This data cannot be linked to your identity and is used solely to improve the stability and performance of the App.

## Cookies and Tracking

The App does not use cookies, analytics tools, advertising networks, or any third-party tracking technologies.

## Device Permissions

The App does not require access to your location, camera, microphone, contacts, or other sensitive device features. The Lock Screen countdown feature requires Live Activities support (iOS 16.1+), which does not transmit any data outside your device.

## Third Parties

No data is shared with third parties. The App does not contain advertisements or social media integrations.

## Data Retention

Since the App does not collect personal data, there is no data retention period to address. Local settings stored on your device can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the App.

## Your Rights

Under the GDPR (EU) 2016/679, you have the following rights in relation to your data:

- **Right to access** — the right to request copies of the information we hold about you.
- **Right to rectification** — the right to have your data corrected if it is inaccurate or incomplete.
- **Right to erasure** — the right to request deletion of your data.
- **Right to restrict processing** — the right to limit the way we use your data.
- **Right to data portability** — the right to request transfer of your data.
- **Right to object** — the right to object to our use of your data.

Since FDP Monitor does not collect any personal data, these rights are practically not applicable. For any inquiries, contact us at daniel.mikes@icloud.com.

## Transfers Outside the EEA

The App does not transfer any data outside the European Economic Area or outside your device.

## Changes to This Policy
