# Privacy Policy — FDP Monitor

**Effective date: March 4, 2026**

## Introduction

This privacy policy applies to the FDP Monitor application (hereinafter "the App"), developed by prodavam-nemovitost.cz, s.r.o. The privacy of your information is taken very seriously. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

## Data controller

The data controller is prodavam-nemovitost.cz, s.r.o., e-mail: daniel.mikes@icloud.com. For the purposes of applicable data protection laws, the data controller determines the purposes and manner in which your data is processed.

## Data collection

The App does not collect any personal data. All FDP calculations, settings, and entered values are processed and stored exclusively on your device. No data is transmitted to the developer's servers.

## Automatically collected data

The App may automatically collect anonymous crash and error data through Apple's services. This data cannot be linked to your identity and is used solely to improve the App's stability.

## Cookies and tracking

The App does not use cookies, analytics tools, advertising networks, or any third-party tracking technologies.

## Device permissions

The App does not require access to your location, camera, microphone, contacts, or other sensitive device features. The Lock Screen countdown feature requires Live Activities support (iOS 16.1+), which does not transmit any data outside your device.

## Third parties

No data is shared with third parties. The App does not contain advertisements or social media integrations.

## Data retention

Since the App does not collect personal data, there is no data retention to address. Local settings on your device can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the App.

## Your rights

Under the GDPR (EU) 2016/679, you have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and the right to object. Since we do not collect personal data, these rights are practically not applicable. For any inquiries, contact us at info@prodavam-nemovitost.cz.

## Transfers outside the EEA

The App does not transfer any data outside the European Economic Area or outside your device.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be made available directly within the App. By continuing to use the App after changes are made, you accept the updated policy.

## Contact

For any questions regarding privacy, contact the developer at info@prodavam-nemovitost.cz.
